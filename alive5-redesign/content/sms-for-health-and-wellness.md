# SMS texting for  health & wellness

Stay connected, effortlessly - SMS enable your office phone line for seamless scheduling and instant patient and member updates.

## Decrease no-shows and filllast minute openings

SMS reminders that can significantly reduce no-show rates by notifying patients of their appointments.

Confirm, reschedule, and send appointment details with ease.

![](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/519d3ac1/dms3rep/multi/opt/offline-1920w.png)

![](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/519d3ac1/dms3rep/multi/opt/text-call-1920w.png)

## Offer a choice to

## “Call or Text”

Patients and members can text to schedule appointments, ask questions about insurance coverage, or request callbacks when lines are busy, offering a convenient and flexible way to interact with your practice​​.

Free up the phone lines and it's a win-win!

## Grow your practice

## ﻿one text at a time

By enhancing patient communication and accessibility, Alive5 helps grow service capacity and responsiveness.

Increase engagement rates from patients, who may be more likely to follow up on appointments, respond to reminders, or participate in health programs.
