AliveCard - Digital Business Cards

The Challenge

* * *

Only 1 out of 10 people will keep your business card.

AliveCard starts a text message every time someone scans your QR code to make sure you're connected to anyone you meet in person.

Stand out at your next networking event with a business card that instantly connects you to a prospect to help you get the next meeting.

Instantly share social links such as LinkedIn™, Facebook™, Instagram™, Youtube™ and more.

Easily add new contacts to your phone and chat instantly.

Reconnect with prospects hours, days, and even weeks after your first meeting, with a 98% open rate.

## FAQs

* * *

Q. Do I have to download anything?

A. Nope! Alivecard makes it super simple - you just have to customize your own personal digital card and add the unique scannable QR code wallpaper to your phone.

Q. How does it work?

A. Once you add the unique QR code wallpaper to your phone, you can ask your prospects to scan it and it will instantly allow them to text you! From there, it will provide a link to your chatbot.

Q. How much does it really cost?

A. Alivecard is completely FREE! That's right.

Optionally, upgrading to a paid version allows you to customize chatbot flows specifically for your organization.

Q. Does this work outside the USA?

A. AliveCard works worldwide. For within the United States, SMS/Text is the preferred messaging option, whereas outside the USA, users can connect via Whatsapp.

Q. Does it work with NFC? Do I need to buy a card?

A. AliveCard is QR code based, so there's no need to buy a physical NFC card, or add a sticker to your phone. Just create a wallpaper with a QR code and set it as your lockscreen.
