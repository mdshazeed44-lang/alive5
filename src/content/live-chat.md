## Keep Track of Customers with Contact Manager

* * *

Each time a conversation starts in Alive5, a new contact is created. When a customer comes back, you'll see their entire history and easily pick up where you left off.

##### On-Brand, Canned Replies

Curate pre-approved templates and scripts for Agents to send to customers - such as greetings, FAQ responses, and more.

##### Mobile & Desktop Apps

Agents can access Alive5 on desktop and mobile so no chat is ever missed. Start a conversation on desktop and finish on mobile, or vice versa.

##### Reporting & Analytics

Measure ROI, monitor performance, and ensure quality using Alive5 real-time analytics tools. Discover your busiest hours, chat volumes, review transcripts, and more.

##### Admin Monitoring & QA

Review and respond to chats in real-time to help with trainees. Capture customer feedback with post chat surveys.
