- ### How accurate is the AI, and what happens if it gives wrong answers?



















Our AI maintains a 70-90% success rate in answering customer questions correctly. The AI is trained exclusively on your business content, which minimizes hallucinations and ensures brand-consistent responses. When the AI isn't confident or gives an incorrect answer, customers can easily escalate to your human team. You'll receive feedback reports showing which questions the AI struggled with, helping you improve accuracy over time.

- ### How long does it take to set up and train the AI?



















Initial setup takes less than 30 seconds - simply upload your content and click "train." Our team handles the complete configuration during your pilot program. Most businesses are answering customer questions within a few days of signing up.

- ### What types of content can I use to train the AI?



















You can train the AI on virtually any text-based content: website pages, PDFs, Word documents, product manuals, pricing guides, FAQ sections, support articles, and custom text responses. The AI works best with well-organized, factual content. We recommend starting with your most frequently accessed support materials and expanding from there.

- ### Can the AI handle multiple languages or only English?



















Currently, our AI primarily supports multi-language interactions. Claude 3.7 Sonnet (our underlying AI model) has capabilities in other major languages. Contact us to discuss specific language requirements - we may be able to accommodate your needs depending on your content and customer base.

- ### How does the AI decide when to escalate to a human agent?



















The AI escalates conversations based on several factors: when it cannot find relevant information in your content, when customers explicitly request human help, when conversations become complex or emotional, or when you've configured specific triggers.





You have full control over escalation rules and can customize them based on your business needs and team availability.

- ### Is my business data secure? Where is it stored?



















AI safety is our top priority. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security measures (Amazon Web Services). We never use your content to train other AI models or share it with other customers. Your business information remains completely private and under your control. We're compliant with major data protection regulations. Please refer to our AI safety and compliance guide for more details.

- ### What happens if I update my website or product information?



















Keeping your AI current is simple. When you update content, just re-upload the new files and click "train" - it takes under 30 seconds.





Customer feedback reports help you identify when information becomes outdated. You can update specific knowledge areas without affecting other parts of your AI's training, making maintenance quick and targeted.

- ### How do I measure ROI and track the AI's performance?



















You'll get detailed analytics showing AI success rates, response times, customer satisfaction, and support workload reduction. Most customers see 70-90% reduction in routine support tickets, faster response times, and improved customer satisfaction. The dashboard tracks cost savings by showing how many interactions were resolved without human intervention.
