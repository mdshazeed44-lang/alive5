### About Us

* * *

Pro and college sports teams, universities, government institutions and SMBs rely on Alive5 for business chat and messaging solutions to engage their audiences, drive high-ticket sales, and provide superior support with a limited staff and IT resources.

"Frankenchat" is a problem we've termed, where organizations try to stitch together multiple vendors for SMS, web chat, social messengers, AI, and automation and integrate their data into CRMs.

Consequently, multiple software platforms (tied together by buggy APIs) are arranged in a mess - excessive browser tabs and app downloads result in disjointed conversations, alerts and notifications overload, and complex team training sessions that further increase their workload.

To program chatbot automation on multiple platforms, IT teams need to learn different chatbot builders. Lastly, integrating each chat channel into a CRM may take months or even years.

Alive5 offers omni-channel audience engagement with one streamlined inbox, one workflow, one set of licenses, and a single CRM integration for all messaging channels. One and Done.

### The Story Behind The Name

* * *

The name "Alive5" is a reference to an 80s movie classic starring a military robot that gained consciousness after being struck by lightning and kept reminding everyone that " [Johnny 5 is Alive](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0091949/?ref_=nv_sr_srsg_0)".
